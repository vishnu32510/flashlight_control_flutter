Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Flashlight Control ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Flashlight Control mobile application.

1) Information We Collect

Flashlight Control is designed to work without sign-in and without server-side accounts. We do not collect personal profile information such as name, email, or phone number.

Local app data

  • Theme preference and similar UI state may be stored locally on your device.
  • No personal data is intentionally transmitted to our servers by the app core feature.

2) Permissions

  • Camera/Flashlight access: Used only to toggle the device torch.

We do not capture, record, or store camera photos/videos for the flashlight feature.

3) Third-Party Services

The app may use open-source packages to access platform flashlight APIs. These packages are used only to provide app functionality and not for advertising profiles.

4) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Since we do not maintain user accounts for this app, there is generally no personal data to share.

5) Data Retention

Any app state is retained locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes local app data managed by the operating system.

6) Security

We follow reasonable practices for app reliability and dependency management. No app or transmission method is completely risk-free.

7) Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates are reflected by changing the "Last updated" date on this page.
